## 📱 What Does This App Do?

The **WhatsApp Message Forwarding App** allows you to:

- ✅ **Automatically forward** WhatsApp messages from one number to another.
- ✅ **Filter messages** — optionally, only forward messages with certain keywords.
- ✅ **Keep a log** of all forwarded messages.

> **In simple words:** Imagine you receive an important WhatsApp message and you want it to be automatically sent to another person — this app does that for you, automatically!

## 📊 WhatsApp Business App vs WhatsApp Cloud API

Many people wonder: *"Do I need a Meta/Facebook account to use this app?"*

Here is a simple comparison:

| | 📱 WhatsApp Business App | 🔌 WhatsApp Cloud API *(This App)* |
|---|---|---|
| **What it is** | A mobile app for small businesses | A developer API for automation |
| **Who manages it** | You (the business owner) | The developer manages it for you |
| **Meta account needed?** | ❌ No | ✅ Yes — but only the **developer** needs it |
| **What YOU need as a user** | Just a phone number | Just your **phone number** ✅ |
| **Best for** | Small shops, freelancers | Automated forwarding, integrations |

> 💡 **Bottom Line:** As an **end user** of this app, **you do NOT need a Meta account**. The developer has already set everything up. **All you need is your phone number!**

## 🚀 How to Get Started (End User)

Getting started is super easy! Just follow these 3 simple steps:

### Step 1: 📞 Provide Your Phone Number

Contact the app administrator (developer) and provide:

- Your **WhatsApp phone number** (with country code, e.g., `+1 234 567 8900`)
- Whether you want to be the **sender** (messages forwarded FROM your number) or the **receiver** (messages forwarded TO your number)

### Step 2: ✅ Confirm Your Number

The developer will add your number to the system. You may receive a **WhatsApp verification message** — simply reply or follow the instructions in that message to confirm.

### Step 3: 🎉 You're Done!

Once your number is added and confirmed, the app will automatically start forwarding messages as configured. No further action needed from your side!

## 📲 How to Use the App

Once set up, the app works **completely in the background** — you don't need to do anything!

### ✅ To Receive Forwarded Messages:
- Just keep WhatsApp open on your phone as usual.
- Forwarded messages will arrive like normal WhatsApp messages.

### 🔄 To Update Your Phone Number:
If you need to **change your phone number**, simply:
1. Contact the app administrator (developer).
2. Provide your **new phone number** (with country code).
3. The developer will update it in the system — usually within a few minutes!

> 📌 **You do not need to do anything technical yourself.** Just inform the developer of your new number.

### 🛑 To Stop Receiving Forwarded Messages:
- Contact the app administrator and ask them to remove your number.

## ❓ Common Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Do I need a Meta or Facebook account?**  
> A: **No!** As an end user, you do NOT need a Meta account. Only the developer/administrator needs one. You just need your phone number. 😊

---

**Q: Do I need to install any special app?**  
> A: **No!** You only need the regular **WhatsApp** app (which you probably already have). No extra apps needed.

---

**Q: How do I change my phone number?**  
> A: Simply contact the app administrator and provide your new phone number with country code (e.g., `+1 234 567 8900`). They will update it for you quickly!

---

**Q: Will I know when a message is forwarded?**  
> A: Yes! Forwarded messages will arrive in your WhatsApp chat. They may show a small **"Forwarded"** label.

---

**Q: Is this app free to use?**  
> A: Yes! The app itself is free for end users. The developer manages any API costs on their side.

---

**Q: Is my phone number safe?**  
> A: Your phone number is only used to send/receive WhatsApp messages within the app. It is stored securely and never shared with third parties.

---

**Q: What happens if my internet goes down?**  
> A: The app will pause forwarding. Once your internet is restored, it will resume automatically. You won't miss any messages.

---

**Q: Can I forward messages to multiple people?**  
> A: Yes! Contact the app administrator — they can configure forwarding to multiple numbers.
