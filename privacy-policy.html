1. Who We Are

WhatsApp Forwarder ("we", "us", "our") is a business messaging tool that connects to the WhatsApp Business Platform (Meta Cloud API) using credentials you provide, and forwards inbound messages to destinations you configure. Our service is operated by the entity listed in the contact section below.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • Connect to Meta's WhatsApp Cloud API using your credentials to receive and forward messages
  • Route inbound messages to the destinations you configure (WhatsApp numbers, email, webhooks)
  • Display message history and usage statistics in your dashboard
  • Send transactional emails (account verification, billing receipts)
  • Enforce plan limits and process subscription billing via Razorpay

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sell it to third parties.

4. WhatsApp / Meta Platform Data

WhatsApp Forwarder accesses the WhatsApp Business Platform through the Meta Cloud API using credentials that you own and control. We act as a data processor on your behalf. Specifically:

  • We receive inbound webhook events from Meta containing message content and sender information
  • We forward that content to destinations you have configured
  • We store message logs for the retention period you configure
  • We do not use WhatsApp message content for any purpose other than executing your forwarding rules
  • We do not share WhatsApp message content with third parties except the destinations you have explicitly configured

Your use of WhatsApp Business Platform is also subject to WhatsApp's Business Policy and Meta's Platform Terms.

6. Data Security

  • WhatsApp credentials (access tokens, app secrets) are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM
  • All data in transit is encrypted via TLS 1.2+
  • Session tokens are stored as SHA-256 hashes — the original token is never persisted
  • Webhook signature verification (HMAC-SHA256) ensures only legitimate Meta events are processed
  • Rate limiting is applied to all authentication endpoints to prevent brute-force attacks
  • The database uses WAL mode with regular integrity checks

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — correct inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data (message logs, workspace configuration, credentials)
  • Portability — receive your message logs in machine-readable format (JSON/CSV)
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Restriction — request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

To exercise any right, email us at the address in Section 11. We will respond within 30 days. Account and data deletion can also be performed directly from your account settings.

8. Data Retention

  • Message logs are retained for 90 days by default. You can clear them at any time from your dashboard.
  • Account data is retained until you delete your account.
  • Workspace credentials are deleted immediately upon workspace deletion.
  • Billing records are retained for 7 years as required by applicable financial regulations.
  • After account deletion, anonymised aggregate usage statistics may be retained for up to 12 months.

9. International Data Transfers

Our service is hosted on servers within the region you select. If data is transferred outside your jurisdiction (for example, to Meta's servers in the United States for WhatsApp API calls), such transfers are governed by Meta's data processing terms and applicable Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards.

11. Cookies and Tracking
